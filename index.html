Our Process

Here at Droitwich Back and Neck Clinic our goal is to help you overcome pain and physical limitations and get back to doing what you love.
We understand how frustrating it can be for you to live with pain, and not being able to fully participate in life. For just a brief moment, imagine what having a body that functions at its very best would be like.
What would be possible for you?
Our Holistic Health Process is how we bridge the gap from where you currently are, to where you want to be. We do this in a caring, patient centered way that leaves you feeling empowered about your health.

We Listen.

Our complimentary consult is a time for us to listen carefully to how your problem affects your daily life. The doctor will take a thorough clinical history and ask about your goals for your health. The purpose of this stage is to clarify the problem and determine whether chiropractic can help you meet your goals.

Examination

Our comprehensive examination process includes neurological, orthopedic, and musculoskeletal tests designed to establish an accurate diagnosis.
