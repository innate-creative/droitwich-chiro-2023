Our Process

{% include chiro-icons.html icon='5' fill_color="green" classes="object-contain w-full h-full" %}

We Listen.

Our complimentary consult is a time for us to listen carefully to how your problem affects your daily life. The doctor will take a thorough clinical history and ask about your goals for your health. The purpose of this stage is to clarify the problem and determine whether chiropractic can help you meet your goals.

{% include chiro-icons.html icon='26' fill_color="green" classes="object-contain w-full h-full" %}

Examination

Our comprehensive examination process includes neurological, orthopedic, and musculoskeletal tests designed to establish an accurate diagnosis.

{% include chiro-icons.html icon='11' fill_color="green" classes="object-contain w-full h-full" %}

Personalized Treatment

Once the doctor has established a diagnosis, he will tailor a treatment plan specific to your needs and your health goals.

{% include chiro-icons.html icon='15' fill_color="green" classes="object-contain w-full h-full" %}

Prevention

When your initial goals have been met, we may recommend periodic maintenance adjustments, specific rehabilitation exercises, or lifestyle changes to help prevent problems from returning.
